Our Story

Colorful resin pieces with a personal heartbeat

Sam’s Nik Naks started as a tiny table at the Harrisburg flea. Today we pour, cure, and polish resin art that captures personal stories for clients across Central Pennsylvania.

Studio ethos

Every pour is intentional. We design to hold memory, whether that’s florals from your wedding bouquet, a favorite color palette, or inclusions sourced from family heirlooms.

We blend hippie warmth with high-end craftsmanship — the vibe is welcoming, but the finish is gallery-ready.

Meet Sam

Sam (they/she) is a resin artist, color nerd, and Camp Hill local. Years of scenic design led to an obsession with micro textures and reflective materials. Resin became the medium for custom keepsakes that glow.

When Sam isn’t pouring, you’ll find them sourcing inclusions from local makers or filming studio sessions for the Join Me video hub.
