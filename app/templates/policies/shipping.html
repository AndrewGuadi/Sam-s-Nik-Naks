Shipping Policy

Orders ship within 3–5 business days unless marked made-to-order. Custom commissions include lead time shared during intake.

Domestic orders ship via USPS Priority or UPS Ground. Tracking numbers are emailed automatically through Stripe when the label prints.

Shipping rates are calculated by weight and destination at checkout. Orders over $200 ship free within Pennsylvania.

Need a rush? Email hello@samsniknaks.com and we’ll review options.
