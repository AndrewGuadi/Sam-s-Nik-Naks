Care Guide

Keep your resin glowing for years

Cleaning

Wipe with a soft microfiber cloth and mild soap. Avoid abrasive scrubbers that can dull the glossy finish.

Heat

Keep resin away from direct heat sources above 120°F. Trays handle warm mugs, but not stovetops.

Sunlight

Display pieces out of prolonged UV to preserve clarity. Occasional sun won’t harm, but avoid weeks of direct rays.

Storage

Store earrings in the provided pouch, and lay trays flat. For dominoes, keep them in their box to maintain their gloss.
