Information We Collect

We collect information to process orders, support you, and improve our site.

Information you provide

  • Account and contact details (name, email, billing/shipping addresses)
  • Order details (items, notes, gift messages)
  • Messages sent via our contact form

Information collected automatically

  • Device and usage data (IP address, browser, pages viewed)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP)
  • Cookies and similar technologies for session and analytics

Information from third parties

  • Payment processors (payment status, limited card details—never full card numbers)
  • Shipping carriers (tracking info, delivery confirmations)
  • Analytics providers (aggregate site performance and usage)

How We Use Information

  • • Process, fulfill, and deliver your orders
  • • Provide support, respond to inquiries, and handle returns
  • • Personalize content and remember preferences
  • • Improve our products, operations, and website performance
  • • Send transactional communications (order, shipping, policy updates)
  • • With your consent, send marketing messages you can opt out of anytime
  • • Comply with legal obligations and enforce policies

How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We share with:

  • Service providers under contract (payment processing, hosting, analytics, shipping)
  • Authorities, when required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Successors in a business transfer, subject to this policy

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies to power the cart, keep you signed in, and measure site performance.

Essential

Required for core functionality like checkout and security.

Performance

Helps us understand traffic, pages visited, and errors.

Marketing

Used (if enabled) to deliver and measure promotions.

You can adjust browser settings to limit or delete cookies. Some features may not work without essential cookies.

Marketing Preferences

You can opt out of marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link in any message. Transactional emails about your orders will still be sent.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as access, correction, deletion, portability, and restriction/objection to processing. You may also have the right to opt out of certain data sharing.

To exercise rights, please contact us. We may verify your request to protect your information.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to our practices or for legal reasons. We will post the updated policy with a new "Last updated" date.
