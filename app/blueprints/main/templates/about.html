Meet the Maker

Hi, I'm Sam. I started Sam's Nik Naks to explore playful color and form through resin. What began as a single batch of earrings has become a weekly ritual of limited drops—small runs with big personality.

I believe everyday objects should feel special: a tray that brightens your desk, a keychain that sparks conversation, a set of dominoes that turns game night into an experience.
