Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your purchase. If something’s not right, we’ll make it right. This page explains how returns, exchanges, and refunds work.

Eligibility How to Start Exchanges Refunds Non‑returnable Return Shipping Damaged Items Late or Missing Refunds Contact

Return Eligibility

  • • Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery
  • • Item must be unused, in original condition and packaging
  • • Proof of purchase is required (order number or receipt)

Note: Due to their personalized nature, custom pieces are final sale unless defective.

How to Start a Return

  1. Locate your order number in your confirmation email.
  2. Contact us via the contact page with your order number and reason.
  3. We’ll reply with a prepaid label (if applicable) and instructions.

Exchanges

Need a different color or style? Exchanges are processed as a return plus a new purchase to ensure availability of limited drops.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we’ll notify you of approval or rejection. Approved refunds are issued to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Non‑returnable Items

  • Gift cards
  • Custom or personalized items (unless defective)
  • Final sale items (marked as such at time of purchase)

Return Shipping

We provide prepaid labels for approved returns within the U.S. Original shipping is non‑refundable unless we made an error. For international returns, shipping is the responsibility of the customer.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos and your order number. We’ll make it right.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received a refund yet, first check with your bank or card issuer—it may take time to post. Then contact us if it’s still missing.

Need help with a return?

We’re here to help. Start your request and we’ll reply within 1–2 business days.
